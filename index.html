JazzEar

Chord Progression Ear Training

Improve your recognition of jazz progressions with a variety of exercises and features:

  • Study from hundreds of hand-curated chord progressions from easy to difficult, covering topics such as diatonic movements, diminished passing chords, modulations, and more.
  • Practice randomly generated chords or 2-5 movements to test difficult intervals and modulations.
  • Try progressions inspired by real jazz tunes, with options to tune the difficulty to your level.
  • Make your best attempt at the "daily song" and compare results with your friends!
  • Stats to track your streaks and how well you performed at each exericse. Aim for fewer mistakes, fewer listens, and faster completion.
